About Me

Hi, I’m Magda, a Kraków-based writer and film professional with academic roots in Intercultural Relations and Film Studies & New Media from Jagiellonian University. I currently work as part of the production team at the Krakow Film Festival and serve as a programmer for the short film section at the Etiuda & Anima International Film Festival.
I specialize in Southeast Asian cinema, focusing on underrepresented narratives and historical resistance in visual media.
I’m deeply interested in countercultural and global youth movements of the 1960s. I especially enjoy discovering films and music from that era and incorporating them into my writing.
